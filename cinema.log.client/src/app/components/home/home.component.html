ELO Rating System

Inspired by the ELO rating system used in chess, Cinema.Log helps you build a more accurate picture of your film preferences over time.

How It Works

1

Compare Films: Review films against others you've already rated. This head-to-head comparison updates their ELO ratings.

2

Dynamic Ratings: Each comparison adjusts the relative rankings of films in your collection based on your preferences.

3

Refined Over Time: The more films you rate and compare, the more accurate your personal film rankings become.

Unlike simple star ratings, ELO gives you a living, evolving ranking that truly reflects your taste in cinema.
